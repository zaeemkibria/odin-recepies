Recepie for Homemade Pasta

Today you will learn how to make Pasta from scratch

Description

Homemade Pasta is a delicious Italian dish that is typically served with sauce and meat use.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • small potatoes, peeled and halved
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • tablespoon minced fresh ginger root

Steps to Follow

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large skillet. Fry potatoes in hot oil until lightly browned, about 3 to 5 minutes
  3. Add remaining 2 tablespoons of oil to the skillet. Add onions, garlic, and fresh ginger;
  4. Add remaining 2 tablespoons of oil to the skillet. Add onions, garlic, and fresh ginger;
